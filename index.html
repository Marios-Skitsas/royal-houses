TWO WELCOMING HOLIDAY HOMES IN STENI

Experience authentic village life with modern comfort. Two lovely 3-bedroom holiday homes each with en suite bathrooms in the heart of Steni village, Cyprus awaits you!

Escape to the tranquil village of Steni, where our two beautifully furnished holiday houses-each with their own private pool offer a peaceful getaway surrounded by nature.

Perfect for families and friends, these fully equipped homes provide comfort, privacy and panoramic views of the surrounding hills and boundless gulf of Polis Chrysochous. Cool off in your own pool, relax on the terrace or take a short trip to the breathtaking Akamas Peninsula known for its crystal-clear beaches, hiking trails and unspoiled landscapes. Just a short drive from Polis Chrysochous and the charming coastal area of Latchi, you’ll have easy access to beaches, restaurants and supermarkets.

Steni itself is a tranquil, authentic Cypriot village with its own restaurants, museum and coffee shop, offering a taste of local life in a peaceful setting.

Whether you are seeking a quiet family holiday or to celebrate a special event, our Steni homes are your perfect getaway!
