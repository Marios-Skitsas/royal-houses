THE ROYAL HOUSES

Prince

Villa Prince, comfortably accommodates up to 6 guests with 3 spacious bedrooms each with its own en suite bathrooms. There are 2 double beds and 2 single beds- spacious enough for families and friends. Nestled in a prime location with breathtaking sunset views, this fully furnished villa offers the perfect setting for unforgettable memories. Enjoy a bold, colorful interior design with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, air conditioning and a fireplace. Relax in the stunning infinity pool overlooking breathtaking views that perfectly captures the golden hour.

Minimum stay is 4 nights (shorter stays are subjected to higher rates).

No pets allowed.
