THE ROYAL HOUSES

Queen

Villa Queen is a tranquil retreat that sleeps up to 6 guests with 2 double beds and 2 single beds, an excellent choice for families or small groups. Each of the 3 stylish bedrooms has its own en suite bathroom. Located in an area renowned for its spectacular sunsets, the villa features a stunning infinity pool perfectly positioned to take in the view. Inside, golden-hued interiors and elegant finishes create a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Guests can enjoy the comfort of air conditioning in the summer and a cozy fireplace in winter, making it a perfect year-round getaway.

A minimum booking of 4 nights is required, with an extra fee applied for shorter stays.

No pets allowed.
